1. Introduction

At Horizon Bank ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our banking services, visit our website, or interact with us through various channels.

This policy applies to all customers, prospective customers, and website visitors. By using our services or providing us with your personal information, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Important Notice

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates and complies with the UAE Data Protection Law and Central Bank of UAE regulations.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with our banking services and improve your experience. The information we collect includes:

Personal Information

  • Full name, date of birth, and nationality
  • Emirates ID, passport, and visa information
  • Contact information (address, phone, email)
  • Employment and income details
  • Financial information and transaction history
  • Investment preferences and risk tolerance

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and platform
  • Website usage patterns and preferences
  • Login credentials and authentication data
Data Category Purpose Legal Basis
Identity Data Account opening, KYC compliance Legal obligation
Financial Data Credit assessment, service provision Contractual necessity
Transaction Data Processing payments, fraud prevention Contractual necessity
Marketing Data Product recommendations, promotions Legitimate interest

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for various purposes related to providing banking services and maintaining our relationship with you:

Primary Uses

  1. Service Provision: Processing transactions, managing accounts, and providing banking products
  2. Risk Management: Assessing creditworthiness, preventing fraud, and monitoring suspicious activities
  3. Regulatory Compliance: Meeting KYC, AML, and other regulatory requirements
  4. Customer Support: Responding to inquiries, resolving issues, and providing assistance
  5. Product Development: Improving existing services and developing new products

Secondary Uses

  • Marketing and promotional activities (with your consent)
  • Market research and analytics
  • System security and fraud prevention
  • Legal proceedings and dispute resolution

4. Information Sharing

We may share your personal information in limited circumstances and only as permitted by law:

Authorized Sharing

  • Regulatory Authorities: Central Bank of UAE, Ministry of Finance, and other regulatory bodies
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in providing our services
  • Credit Bureaus: For credit reporting and risk assessment purposes
  • Legal Requirements: When required by court orders or legal proceedings
  • Business Partners: With your explicit consent for specific services

Data Sharing Principles

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. All data sharing is governed by strict contracts and security requirements.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Technical Safeguards

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Advanced firewall and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

Operational Safeguards

  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Role-based access controls and need-to-know basis
  • Regular security awareness programs
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

6. Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access Rights

  • Request copies of your personal information
  • Ask about how your data is being used
  • Request information about data sharing

Control Rights

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of certain data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to certain processing activities

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer using the information provided at the end of this policy. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Cookies & Technology

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your online experience:

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: 10 years after account closure (as required by UAE banking law)
  • Transaction Records: 7 years for audit and compliance purposes
  • Marketing Data: Until you opt-out or 3 years of inactivity
  • Website Data: As per our cookie policy or until deletion requested

9. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to countries outside the UAE for specific purposes:

  • Processing by our international service providers
  • Compliance with global regulatory requirements
  • Providing international banking services

We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by UAE authorities
  • Binding Corporate Rules for group companies

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify you of material changes via email or website notice
  • Provide 30 days' notice before significant changes take effect
  • Maintain previous versions for reference
  • Highlight key changes in update notifications
