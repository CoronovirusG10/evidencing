Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I open a bank account?

Opening an account with Horizon Bank is simple. You can apply online through our website, visit any of our branches, or call our customer service. You'll need your Emirates ID, passport, salary certificate, and proof of address. The process typically takes 24-48 hours for approval.

What are your customer service hours?

Our customer service is available 24/7 through our hotline 800 HORIZON. Branch hours are Monday to Friday 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Digital branches in malls are open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

How do I activate Internet Banking?

You can activate Internet Banking instantly through our website or mobile app. Use your account number and Emirates ID to register. You'll receive an OTP on your registered mobile number to complete the activation. For assistance, call our 24/7 helpline.

What should I do if I lose my card?

Immediately block your card through our mobile app, Internet Banking, or by calling 800 SECURE. You can request a replacement card online or at any branch. Emergency cash and temporary card services are available for urgent needs.

How can I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan online through our website, mobile app, or visit any branch. We offer personal loans, home loans, auto finance, and more. You'll need to provide salary certificate, bank statements, and Emirates ID. Get instant pre-approval online in minutes.

What are the fees for international transfers?

International transfer fees vary based on the amount and destination. Standard transfers start from AED 50. We offer competitive exchange rates and same-day transfers to most countries. Premium account holders enjoy reduced fees and priority processing.
