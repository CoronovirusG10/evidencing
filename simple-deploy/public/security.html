Security Best Practices

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Change them regularly and never share with others.

Secure Networks

Always use secure, private networks for banking. Avoid public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.

Regular Monitoring

Check your account statements regularly and report any suspicious transactions immediately.

Software Updates

Keep your devices and browsers updated with the latest security patches.

Phishing Awareness

Be cautious of suspicious emails, texts, or calls asking for personal information.

Safe Logout

Always log out completely from your banking session and close your browser.
