About Horizon Bank

Building lasting relationships through innovative banking solutions and exceptional service since 1985

Pioneering Banking Excellence in the UAE

For nearly four decades, Horizon Bank has been at the forefront of banking innovation in the United Arab Emirates, providing comprehensive financial solutions to individuals, businesses, and institutions across the region.

Our Story

Established in 1985, Horizon Bank began as a vision to create a financial institution that would bridge traditional banking values with modern innovation. From our humble beginnings with a single branch in Dubai, we have grown to become one of the UAE's most trusted banking partners.

Today, we operate over 50 branches across the Emirates, serving more than 2 million customers with a comprehensive range of banking products and services. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous awards and recognitions in the banking industry.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted banking partner in the UAE by delivering innovative financial solutions that empower our customers to achieve their goals and aspirations. We strive to create value for all stakeholders while contributing to the economic growth and prosperity of the communities we serve.

We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust, transparency, and mutual success. Our customer-centric approach ensures that every interaction adds value and exceeds expectations.
